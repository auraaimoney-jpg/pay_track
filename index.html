Getting Started

How do I add a project?
Click "Add Project" button, fill in the project name, deployed date, payment period, and monthly amount.
How does the countdown work?
The countdown timer shows time remaining until the next payment due date based on the deployment date and period.
How is UGX calculated?
UGX is calculated using the exchange rate set in Settings (default: 1 USD = 3,750 UGX).
